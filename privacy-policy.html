1. Introduction

ASHIDA ELECTRONICS (“we”, “our”, or “us”) developed the mobile application ASHIDA ESS BETA as an Employee Self Service (ESS) application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

The app collects and uses your location information only for:

  • Employee attendance
  • Geo-tagging check-in/check-out
  • Validating presence at workplace or assigned location

We do not track your location in the background. Location is collected only when required during check-in/check-out.

2.2 Device Information

The app collects device information for security and device binding purposes:

  • Device ID (unique identifier generated from device characteristics)
  • Device model and brand
  • Operating system and version
  • Device name

This information is used to ensure your account is accessed only from your registered device, preventing unauthorized access and enhancing security.

2.3 Personal and Employment Information

The app processes the following information provided by your employer:

  • Employee ID and name
  • Username and authentication credentials
  • Attendance records
  • Leave applications and history
  • Holiday information
  • Profile and employment details

2.4 Network and Connection Information

The app requires internet access to communicate with the company's HR system. We collect network state information to ensure proper connectivity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Attendance Management: Recording and tracking employee check-in/check-out with location data
  • Device Security: Binding your account to your registered device to prevent unauthorized access
  • Authentication: Verifying your identity and maintaining secure access to the app
  • HR Operations: Processing leave applications, viewing attendance history, and managing employee records
  • Service Delivery: Providing access to company announcements, holidays, and profile information

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or external third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared only with:

  • Your organization's HR/Administration team
  • Authorized internal systems of ASHIDA ELECTRONICS

We do not share your information with external parties unless required by law.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely on company servers and on your device in encrypted secure storage. We retain your information for as long as you are an active employee or as required by law and company policy. Device information is stored only on your registered device.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted secure storage for credentials on your device
  • HTTPS encrypted communication with company servers
  • Device binding to prevent unauthorized account access
  • Restricted access to HR data within the company

However, no electronic method is 100% secure. Please keep your device secure and do not share your credentials.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where applicable
  • Withdraw permission for location access (some features may stop working)

To request changes, contact us at: abs-support@ashidaelectronics.com

8. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo Services: For app development framework and updates
  • Your Company's Frappe/ERPNext Server: For HR data management

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended only for registered employees of ASHIDA ELECTRONICS. It is not intended for children under 18.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page or notified through the app.
